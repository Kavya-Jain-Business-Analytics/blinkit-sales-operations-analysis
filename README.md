# Blinkit Sales & Operations Analysis

## Project Overview

This project is a business analytics project focused on analysing Blinkit sales performance, product-level trends, outlet characteristics, and operational performance using Microsoft Excel and Microsoft Power BI.

The project involved working with retail and outlet-level data, preparing the dataset for analysis, performing Excel-based analysis using PivotTables and dashboards, and developing a Power BI dashboard to generate meaningful business insights.

The analysis demonstrates how raw business data can be transformed into structured analysis and visual dashboards to support performance evaluation and data-driven decision-making.

---

## Project Objective

The primary objective of this project was to analyse sales and outlet performance across multiple business dimensions.

The analysis focused on understanding:

- Overall sales performance
- Product category contribution
- Outlet-level performance
- Outlet type performance
- Outlet size performance
- Location-based performance
- Customer ratings
- Outlet establishment trends
- Key business performance indicators

The project was designed to transform raw retail data into meaningful business insights using Excel and Power BI.

---

## Key Analytical Areas

### 1. Sales Performance Analysis

Analysis of overall sales performance and revenue contribution across different business dimensions.

The analysis helps evaluate the overall performance of the retail business and identify major contributors to sales.

---

### 2. Product Category Analysis

Analysis of product categories based on their sales contribution and performance.

This helps identify:

- High-performing product categories
- Lower-performing product categories
- Product-level sales patterns
- Category contribution to overall business performance

---

### 3. Outlet Performance Analysis

Comparison of outlet performance across different outlet characteristics.

The analysis helps understand how outlet-level factors may be associated with differences in business performance.

---

### 4. Outlet Type Analysis

Comparison of performance across different outlet types.

This provides insights into:

- Outlet type contribution
- Sales performance by outlet type
- Differences in outlet-level performance

---

### 5. Outlet Size Analysis

Analysis of business performance across different outlet sizes.

This helps evaluate whether outlet size is associated with differences in sales performance.

---

### 6. Location Analysis

Comparison of outlet performance across different location tiers.

The analysis provides insights into how outlet location may influence business performance.

---

### 7. Customer Rating Analysis

Analysis of customer ratings in relation to outlet and business performance.

This helps understand customer feedback patterns and their relationship with performance indicators.

---

### 8. Establishment Trend Analysis

Analysis of outlet establishment patterns over time.

This helps identify trends in outlet development and business expansion.
